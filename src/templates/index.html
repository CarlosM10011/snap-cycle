What is Safe Cycle?

Safe Cycle serves as a guide to teach people the proper way to dispose of any item by reducing confusion about where certain items go. To get started, simply search an item in the search bar loacted on the top right of the page.

Why do we need to Recycle?

Recycling is important because extensive amounts of waste can have negative impacts on our environment. In the recent years where recycling has become more prevalent, the US consistently ranks last in recycling rates. Top countries in Europe such as Germany, recycle 70% of all their wastes while the United States lags behind at 34%.

Our Mission

Our mission is to help promote awareness about recycling and educate people on how to dispose of any item. Learning how to reduce, reuse, and recycle correctly are steps we can take to help make our Earth a greener, cleaner place.
